# Create a project

## Create a consortium project

1. Click **Create project**.
1. Click **Consortium**.
1. Provide **Project name** and optionally **Description**.
1. Click **Create**.

## Create a public chain project

1. Click **Create project**.
1. Click **Public chain**.
1. Provide **Project name** and optionally **Description**.
1. Click **Create**.
